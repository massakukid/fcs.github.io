Caregiver Jobs - Caregivers and Companions Needed

Caregivers Vote Visiting Angels The Best Place to Work
We know how important our caregivers are. They are the backbone of our business. Without our team of compassionate caregivers, we wouldn’t be able to help our clients the way we do. We try to make a difference for everyone we are involved with; the people who use our service, our staff and our strategic partners. The approach is one based on open and honest communication, transparency and the highest professional standards aiming to making a difference in caring at home.
If you’re keen to be involved in a caring role that provides quality support to people at home, and you have compassion for individuals in a diverse range of situations, we would really like you to join our growing team.

What FCS can offer.

  • Supportive working environment.
  • Full training to undertake the role and meet legislation.
  • Flexible working hours available, if required.
  • Training towards a recognised qualification in care.
  • Competitive rates of pay (please contact our office for enquiries).
﻿Previous experience would be an advantage but it’s not essential. Comprehensive and paid training will be provided for you during the first two weeks of the recruitment process.
The job role requires you to: provide personal care, perform domestic duties, promote independence, dignity & well-being and generally support people who use our service in their own homes.
Driving license and own transport would be advantageous if you’re looking for a greater number of hours. Successful applicants are required to provide a DBS Enhanced Disclosure.
Facilitate Care Services Limited is an Equal Opportunities employer.

Caregiver roles and responsibilities.

The role of the care/support worker is to: Promote the opportunity for service users to live in the community as long as possible, by providing care and support to the individuals and their families, and to enable service users to live as independently, comfortably and securely as possible.​

We expect our care/support workers to take individual responsibility for the delivery of personal and practical care needs of service users, in line with the practices, procedures and policies of Facilitate Care Services.​

The post holder will undertake all care work with the sensitivity and empathy required to provide services in a way which will preserve the dignity, privacy, choice, independence, fulfilment and rights of the service user and his/her usual carer/members of family.​

Please note that we fully vet and check all our employees’ credentials and a criminal disclosure is undertaken prior to commencement of employment at a fee.​

It will also be necessary for us to obtain two written references with one being from your current or most recent employer.
  • Assisting clients with personal care services such as oral care, bathing, toileting, dressing, and grooming
  • Medication reminders
  • Light housekeeping including laundry, dishes and vacuuming
  • Planning, preparing and serving meals
  • Assistance with transfers, walking and physical activity
  • Assistance with transportation to appointments, activities, errands, and shopping
  • Providing companionship and cheerful, positive assistance at all times
